ABOUT ASLearn

Welcome to NeoSpeaks where learning sign language becomes fun, interactive, and effortless!

At NeoSpeaks, we believe communication is for everyone. That's why we created a tool that helps you learn and practice sign language anytime, anywhere. Using cutting-edge machine learning, our app can detect your hand shapes as you spell out words, giving real-time feedback to help you improve with confidence.

Whether you're learning to connect with someone special or for personal growth, our app makes practicing simple and enjoyable.

Our team of passionate educators, designers and developers are committed to making sign language accessible, engaging, and fun. We've designed NeoSpeaks with a clean, intuitive interface, helpful tutorials (coming soon), and thoughtful guidance. We have everything you need to start your learning journey.

Join us and discover a new way to speak. Let's make sign language part of everyday life!
